HONORARY BOARD OF GOVERNORS

The Board of Governors of SOS Children’s Villages Pakistan comprises of influential individuals from diverse backgrounds including businessmen, lawyers, engineers, social workers, ex senators, bankers and architect, who usher valuable experience to the table. The role of the Board includes governance and direction in all the matters of SOS.

SOS PRESIDENT
The President of SOS Children’s Villages International assumes overall leadership of the global federation of 117 member associations, supporting consistency and cohesion. He leads the International Senate and ensures a common approach among the governance bodies of the Senate, Management Council and Management Team. The President represents the federation externally and internally. Siddhartha Kaul was elected President of SOS Children’s Villages International in June 2012, and re-elected for a second four-year term in June 2016. His association with the organisation goes back to his childhood in India where, in 1964, his father became the Founder and Director of the first SOS Children’s Village in India after meeting Founder Hermann Gmeiner.
